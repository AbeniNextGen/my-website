🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to raise a generation of spiritually grounded students who live out the love, truth, and teachings of Jesus Christ in their daily lives.

  • Share the Gospel of Christ across campuses and communities
  • Strengthen students in prayer, fellowship, and Biblical study
  • Equip young believers to lead with faith and integrity
  • Serve others through love, generosity, and compassion

📖 Our Background

Christian Students General Fellowship was formed by a group of devoted students who shared a passion for worship, evangelism, and helping others know Christ.

Since our founding, we’ve hosted:

  • Bible study sessions and prayer nights
  • Outreach programs in schools and neighborhoods
  • Youth leadership trainings and worship events
  • Social projects to support those in need

With God’s help—and your generous support—we continue this journey, reaching more lives every day.
